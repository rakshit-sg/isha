What We Offer

Paths to Inner Wellbeing

Ancient practices made available to all — guided by trained Isha volunteers with love and commitment.

☀️

Surya Shakthi

An active form of Sun Salutation, this 21-step yogic practice builds physical strength, mental clarity, and vibrance — accessible to all from age 7.

🌿

Inner Engineering

A 7-day transformative program that introduces Shambhavi Mahamudra Kriya — a powerful practice for health, joy, and inner clarity.

🌈

Children's Hatha Yoga

A joyful, age-appropriate yoga program that nurtures children's bodies and minds, fostering balance, focus, and self-awareness from an early age.

🧘

Miracle of Mind

A guided meditation experience that takes you beyond the surface of thought into the depths of stillness — offered freely as a gift of inner peace.

About Us

Isha OMR –
A Volunteer Heart

The Isha Yoga Center – OMR is a dedicated volunteer-run center serving the communities along Old Mahabalipuram Road in Chennai. Every class, every program, and every smile here is offered freely by passionate volunteers who have experienced the power of inner transformation firsthand.

We are guided by the vision of Sadhguru — to offer every human being the tools they need to experience life to its fullest potential. Our center brings time-tested yogic practices to your neighborhood, making inner wellbeing accessible to all.
