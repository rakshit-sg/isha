About Us

Isha OMR —
A Volunteer Heart

The Isha Yoga Center – OMR is a fully volunteer-run center serving the communities along Old Mahabalipuram Road in Chennai. Every class and every program here is offered freely by dedicated volunteers who have experienced the power of inner transformation.

Guided by the vision of Sadhguru, our center brings time-tested yogic practices directly to your neighborhood — making inner wellbeing accessible to everyone regardless of background or experience.
